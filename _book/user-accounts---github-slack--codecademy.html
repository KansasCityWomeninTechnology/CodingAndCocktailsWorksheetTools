User Accounts: GitHub, Slack & Codecademy

Handy User Accounts

Coding & Cocktails supports your own efforts to learn to code. Therefore, in between sessions, you will want to continue to practice on your own. We refer to these sites often in the sessions as critical tools that we use to help learn to code. The accounts are free unless you choose to buy a premium subscription but that is not necessary for anything we do here.

  • Github (https://github.com/) - this is the essential cloud-based companion tool to Git.

  • Codecademy (https://www.codecademy.com) - provides easy to follow, hands-on lessons on many of the topics covered in class.

  • Slack - our 24x7 chat room is kcwit.slack.com The #codingandcocktails channel will help you keep in touch with all of your Coding & Cocktails girlfriends. However, an administrator has to invite you so if you did not receive an invitation, please email us at codingandcocktails@kcwomenintech.org and we’ll take care of you.
